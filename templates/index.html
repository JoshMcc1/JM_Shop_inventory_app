Vehicle Showroom inventroy app

This application is to track and update product and manufacturer details.


The user can view stock levels, the app will indicate low levels of stock and no stock. The user can also filter results by type of vehicle and manufacturer name.

Basic functions included are creating, deleting and updating products and manufacturers as well as displaying individual information for each product and manufacturer

The user can also close off manufacturers, doing this will cause all products from that manufacturer not to be displayed.
